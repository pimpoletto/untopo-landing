# Data retention policy

Last updated: 2026-06-12

## Account and reports

Account and report data are retained for as long as the account remains active, unless the user requests deletion or a legal obligation requires otherwise.

## PDF exports

Stored PDF exports are retained for as long as the associated report exists, or until deleted by the user.

## Logs and observability

Technical logs and observability data are retained for a limited period proportionate to incident detection needs.

## Billing

Billing data required for accounting and tax obligations is retained according to applicable legal time limits.
