# Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-06-12

## 1. Data controller

The Untopo service is published by an entity whose full contact details will be published in the legal notice.

Contact: contact@untopo.com

## 2. Data processed

Untopo processes in particular:

- account data (email, credentials, profile);
- report data (transcribed voice notes, photos, annotations, PDF exports);
- personalization data (company name, logo, signature);
- technical data (logs, diagnostics);
- billing data (subscription status via Stripe).

## 3. Purposes

Data is processed to provide the application, create and export reports, manage subscriptions, and secure the service.

## 4. Subprocessors

Data may be processed by Supabase, Vercel, OpenAI, Resend, Stripe, and Sentry, to the extent necessary for their respective services.

## 5. Individual rights

Under the GDPR, you may request access to, rectification, erasure, or restriction of your data by writing to contact@untopo.com.
